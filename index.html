How to play: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one!

Note: This site is a fork of the official version of 2048. You can play it on your phone via http://kent.doddsfamily.us/2048. This fork of the original adds the ability to login to third parties to keep your high score.

Original created by Gabriele Cirulli. Based on 1024 by Veewo Studio and conceptually similar to Threes by Asher Vollmer.
This fork created by Kent C. Dodds using Firebase for storage and authentication.
